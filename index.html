Intro

I am Ali Rajaii a full-stack Web-Developer. Originally I studied psychology and finance. I started a few businesses to make money after school, but was not satisfied because I felt I was not engaging my creative or intellectual side at all. That changed as I put my entire life on the line to learn how to code. I'm still on the learning path, and always will be in this field. It keeps me sharp and scintillates both my intellect, and my creativity. Click the github link below to see some of my work.

About

I take interest in many aspects of software development. Of particular interest are web-development and computer science. Currently, I have completed a few projects, and I am working on 2 a major projects. One is a sharing-economy application that will serve as platform for a specified target user-group. The other is a technology recruiting platform that I am working on at a startup for my fellowship.

In my personal life I enjoy many wonderful hobbies. I am a martial artist, a musician, a social being, an avid reader, and a spiritual seeker. I find writing software to be very helpful with the spiritual process believe it or not. It forces me to be more mindful. If I am not in a pseudo-meditative state when coding, I will not solve the problems the codebase faces. This is important for me. I can be fully present while doing the things that I love to do best. I am still early in my career as a software engineer, but I will post my successes on here as I progress for all to see. Thank you for taking the time to visit my site.
